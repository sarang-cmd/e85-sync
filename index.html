How to play

  • Left Arrow or left pad = left lane.
  • Right Arrow or right pad = right lane.
  • Tap notes at the hit line near the bottom.
  • For hold notes, press near the head and keep holding comfortably; release timing is lenient.
  • Pause with Space or Escape, restart with R.
  • Use the debug panel to tune BPM, offset, hit windows, density, quantization, seek position, and more.
Put audio at ./assets/audio/song.mp3 or ./assets/audio/song.wav. If audio is missing, the game still works in silent mode. If local browser file restrictions block chart loading, use the file pickers on the right to load chart and audio manually.
